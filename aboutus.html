Who are HEJA?

HEJA Hosting is a server hosting website created and maintained by 4 Norwegian IT-students. It started as a school project, but we are aiming to continue this business after we graduate.


Why choose us?

HEJA Hosting continues to improve and upgrade all the time. Out goal is to give you the best server hosting experience you can get. We are always looking for new ways to make our service better. We believe that success will come as long as we continue to improve and try our best.
